Dama is a luxury women's handmade fashion brand in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The focus of the brand are coats made out of high-quality materials like cashmere and wool. All coats are made with high attention to detail, and some are even custom-made for some of the customers with every part designed and created just for them.​​​​​​​

Delivery is an important part as well. Ordered coats are delivered personally in most of the cities in Bosnia and to show gratitude, each packaging has a rose included.​​​​​​​

The brand started as an idea when I reconnected with my mom during the COVID-19 times. Quick backstory, my mum is an experienced tailor with more than 20 years of experience in women's fashion. Her work was affected by the coronavirus as many of us were. After spending more time with her, I noticed a lot of potential for a great digital business. I tried to connect the knowledge she had with my skills in design and my humble business skills. Dama is a luxury women's handmade fashion brand in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
